Caring for Puppies

Caring for your puppy properly is extriemly important. You must do all things below to be caring for your puppy properly! When you have a puupy you have to give it lots of love every day. Make sure to play with your puppy and never leave it alone. Always take your puppy for walks.
Make sure your puppy has all its shots. If your puppy has hair, brush it and cut it regularly Cut your puppies nails regularly. Give your puppy lots of cuddles. Make sure your puppy is on a healthy diet Make sure your puppy always has water.
Otherwise you are neglecting your puppy!!!
