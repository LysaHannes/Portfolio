Takeout Picker App

Overview

I thought I would work on a small project for myself while job searching. I sometimes have trouble picking where I want to go out to eat so I decided to make a simple webapp using the mern stack to help me out. It will be mobile based cus thats where im going to use this webapp, but I will be making the app responsive.

Im hoping this project will help display my skills as a software engineer/developer.

Here are the curent phases of the development process I have covered so far.
- Planning
- Design

Planning

Figma is a good tool that I have experience using in my other projects and is well known to be used for the planning and deign process of software development, so I decided to use that. This was my initial plan/outline for what I wanted the app to be/have. As I wemt through the design process found I had to add couple more pages.

Design

I am currently still working on the design/layout for the app. But here is what I have so far. (Designs created using Figma)

Login Page:
This will be the defult page if you arent logged in.

Sign Up Page:
This will be the page the comes up is you click sign up on the login page.

Verification Page:
This will be the page the comes up when signing up or loggin as a 2 factor authentication to ensure security.
(The text on the button will be deffernt depending on whether your logging in or signing up)

Home Page:
This will be the page the comes up when you log in. when you click the button it will pick a location from your list of locations.
Additionally the Menu will be acessible from anywhere once logged in.
Im thinking of adding a page for browsing locations to add to your list.

Later on I might add pages for Tutorials, About the App, and Making a Donation. But not now cus they are not important for the functionality of the app.

Profile Page:
This will be the page shows your profile, how many followers you have and the location you have on your list.
If your looking at your own profile, you can delete your locations here.
If your looking at someone elses profile, then you can save one of thier locations to add to your list.

Maybe I will make it so it will show how many people have saved it, and the creator of the location. But I will figure that out when I design the database.

Friends List Page:
This will be the page shows your friends (only you can see this).

Next Pages:
- Add New location
- Settings
- Location was picked

Next Steps

Next I will be finishing up the remaining pages for the apps design, setting up a jira board for all the tasks, then creating an er diagram to design the database.
